Training Programs

Three pathways, one standard of excellence

Choose the pathway that suits your dog, your lifestyle, and your level of involvement — every successful team qualifies as a reliable, task-trained assistance dog.

Quick start

Take our 60-second program quiz

Answer a few quick questions and we'll recommend the training pathway that fits your situation — no need to read through everything below first.

Option 1

Owner-Trained Adult Dog Program

Best for: clients who already own a suitable adult dog or rescue dog, pending suitability assessments.

12–18 monthstypical dog age
Instructor-ledintensive structure

An intensive, instructor-led program focused on developing the dog into a reliable working assistance dog. A qualified instructor trains the dog directly, then teaches the client how to confidently work with and maintain the dog's skills.

Option 2

Owner-Trained Puppy Program

Best for: clients starting with a young puppy external to the organisation.

18–24 monthsapprox. duration
Close collaborationwith an instructor

The client works closely with an instructor throughout the puppy's development, from foundation training through to advanced assistance work.

Important: clients are responsible for maintaining regular daily and weekly practice between instructor sessions.

Option 3

Organisation-Trained Assistance Dog Program

Best for: clients wanting a more supported and comprehensive option.

We take responsibility for sourcing, raising, socialising, and training the dog before matching them to the client's specific needs and lifestyle. The final stage includes client training, team integration, and a structured handover process.

Payment plans up to 18 months Ongoing support Virtual or in-person assessments Rescue dogs welcomed
